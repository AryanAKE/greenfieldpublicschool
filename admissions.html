Admissions

Join the Greenfield family. Discover our admissions process, key dates, and tuition information.

How to Apply

Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and welcoming. Follow these steps to begin your journey at Greenfield Public School.

1

Inquiry & Campus Tour

Submit an online inquiry form and schedule a campus tour to experience our vibrant community firsthand.

2

Submit Application

Complete our online application and submit all required documents, including academic records and recommendations.

3

Assessment & Interview

Prospective students will participate in an age-appropriate assessment and a family interview with our admissions team.

Tuition & Fees

We are committed to providing an exceptional education that is both high-quality and accessible. For a detailed breakdown of tuition, fees, and financial aid opportunities, please download our official fee structure or contact the admissions office directly.

Our team is available to guide you through the financial aid process and answer any questions you may have about affording a Greenfield education.
