Our Academics

Explore our comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare students for the future.

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Our Primary School program focuses on building a strong foundation in core subjects like literacy, numeracy, and science. We foster a love for learning through hands-on activities, collaborative projects, and a supportive environment that encourages curiosity and creativity. Our goal is to develop confident and inquisitive learners.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

During these transitional years, our curriculum encourages students to think critically and take greater ownership of their learning. We offer a balanced program of academics, arts, and athletics, helping students explore their interests and develop essential skills in problem-solving, communication, and collaboration. Leadership and character development are key focuses.

High School (Grades 9-12)

Our High School program prepares students for success in college and beyond. We offer a rigorous academic curriculum, including Advanced Placement (AP) courses, specialized STEM and humanities tracks, and comprehensive college counseling. Students are empowered to pursue their passions, engage in meaningful research, and become responsible global citizens.
